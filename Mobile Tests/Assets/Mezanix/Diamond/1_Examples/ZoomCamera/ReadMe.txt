ZoomCamera

Use:
Open the scene ZoomCamera, play it, click in the game window.
It is a simple scene with a cube and a camera zooming out and in
at the right mouse click.

Important features used (see the ExZoom graph):
1. Go To State: make transitions between states.
2. Set Field Of View for the camera.
3. Get Mouse Button Down: get the right click input.
4. Make a GameObject field 'The Attached To One' telling to
	the generated script to act on the gameobject holding it.
