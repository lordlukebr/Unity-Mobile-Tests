ClickTheSphere

Use:
Open the scene ClickTheSphere, play it. A sphere will move,
if you click on it, you will increment your score.

Important features used (see the ExClickTheSphere graph):
1. Trigger action at the game start.
2. Manipulate Vectors.
3. Random a vector on a circle.
4. Increment int variable.
5. Apply an impulse force to a rigidbody.
6. Use 'Screen Point To Ray' with a camera, a ray starting at
	a point in the screen and going to the world.
