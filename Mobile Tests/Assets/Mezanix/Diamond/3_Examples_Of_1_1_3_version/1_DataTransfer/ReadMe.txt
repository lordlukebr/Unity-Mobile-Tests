DataTransfer
Use:
Open the scene ExDataTransfer, play it, click in the game view, 
press the 'S' key to send your data (Vector3 in this example),
press the 'L' key to listen to transferred data and the value of the
transferred vector will be shown on the screen.

Important features used (see the ExDataTransfer graph):
1. The new data transfer feature of diamond 1.1.3
